# AI-Assisted Multi-Physics Sustainability Evaluation for Traction Inverters

## Summary

This paper integrates sustainability indicators (carbon footprint, lifecycle impact) into traction inverter design using AI-assisted multi-physics evaluation. It addresses the gap in standardized sustainability assessment frameworks for power electronics.

## Key Focus Areas

- **Mission profile-based evaluation** — design assessed against real-world drive cycles (WLTP, EPA)
- **Multi-physics coupling** — electrical + thermal + mechanical evaluated simultaneously
- **AI/ML for sustainability metrics** — predicts lifecycle impact from design parameters
- **Gap addressed:** No standardized sustainability framework exists for power electronics design

## Relevance to SRTP

1. **Directly relevant domain** — traction inverter design evaluation
2. **Multi-physics coupling** validates our claim that AI excels at simultaneous multi-domain optimization
3. **Mission profile-based** — reinforces that drive-cycle evaluation (not just single-point efficiency) matters
4. **Sustainability dimension** — adds a metric (carbon footprint) our current success metrics don't include
5. **AI assistance pattern** — AI augments, doesn't replace, physics-based evaluation
