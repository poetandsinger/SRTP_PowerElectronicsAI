C7 SHORT-CIRCUIT (850 V, hard switch fault, Tj hot) — datasheet/analytic (loss model CANNOT do SC):
  The MosfetWithDiode is a constant-Ron loss model with NO gm-saturation (verified: no transfer table in the
  CAB450 XML). A hard SC would compute 850/0.0036 = 236 kA (unphysical). Bounded from the FM3-family anchor
  (protection-and-safety.md sec.3, [110][126]) scaled to CAB450 by die area (Ron 16/2.6 = 4.44x):
    ID,sat ~ 4.7 kA (~10x rated 450 A); SCWT ~2.73 us @ 850 V/175 C (measured 2.9 us @ 800 V, voltage-scaled;
    < 3 us SiC). DESAT (300 ns) + soft turn-off (1 us) = 1.3 us reaction, inside SCWT with 2.1x margin -> a
    single SC is SURVIVED. Turn-off overvoltage dV = Lsig*di/dt: soft 63 V (18% of BV headroom), hard 313 V
    (89% - uncomfortably close) -> soft turn-off strongly preferred. PASS. Scaling flagged +/-30%.

C8 ASC ENTRY (short all low-side at max speed) — analytic dq (bench cannot model ASC; back-EMF-source machine):
  Steady ASC current bounded at Ich = lam/Ld = 611 A across all speeds. Entry transient (RK4 dq integration
  from motoring at current limit, 16500 rpm) peaks 1924 A = 3.1x steady, then decays -> EXCEEDS device I_DM
  (900 A, 214%): flags staged/hybrid ASC to damp entry, or transient-SOA verification. Drag torque peaks
  235 N.m near 100 rpm, falls ~1/w (controlled braking). NO bus overvoltage: fault current circulates in the
  low-side switches + machine, never reaches the DC-link cap (dV_bus ~ 0). Contrast uncontrolled freewheel:
  back-EMF 1317 V(LL pk) > 850 V bus -> rectifies through body diodes -> BUS PUMPING. PASS: ASC is the correct
  high-speed safe state (freewheel only safe at low speed). protection-and-safety.md sec.5.
